A floating sculpture of a helmet crafted from recycled concrete and plaster, its form a dynamic interplay of organic curves and angular edges. The surface is textured with intricate patterns reminiscent of tree bark, symbolizing resilience and growth. Embedded within the structure are fragments of reclaimed glass, catching light and casting subtle reflections, evoking the delicate balance between nature and industry. The helmet's visor is an open lattice of interwoven vines, suggesting adaptability and interconnectedness. The overall design critiques consumer culture, emphasizing the importance of ecological harmony and resourcefulness.
